Conclusion & Q&A

Our Company's Responsibilities & Your Role

Our Company's Responsibilities (as Data Fiduciary)

Adhere to DPDP Act Principles: Ensure all personal data processing aligns with principles of consent, purpose limitation, data minimization, accuracy, storage limitation, and security.

Implement Robust Security Measures: Apply appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal data from breaches and misuse.

Respect Data Principal Rights: Establish clear processes for individuals to exercise their rights (access, correction, erasure, grievance redressal).

Ensure Timely Breach Notification: Have a clear incident response plan to notify the Data Protection Board and affected individuals in case of a data breach.

Vendor Due Diligence: Ensure all Data Processors working on our behalf comply with DPDP Act requirements through appropriate contracts and oversight.

Training & Awareness: Conduct regular training sessions like this one to ensure all employees understand their data privacy obligations.

Grievance Redressal: Maintain an accessible mechanism for Data Principals to raise concerns.

Your Individual Role in Data Protection

Understand & Comply with Policies: Familiarize yourself with our company's data privacy policies and procedures.

Collect Only Necessary Data: Only collect personal data that is essential for your work purpose.

Process Data Lawfully: Ensure you have a valid reason (e.g., consent, contract) before processing any personal data.

Protect Data Securely:

Use strong, unique passwords and multi-factor authentication.

Lock your screen when away from your desk.

Be cautious of phishing attempts and suspicious emails.

Do not share personal data inappropriately (e.g., via insecure channels, with unauthorized individuals).

Encrypt sensitive data when transferring or storing.

Dispose of physical documents containing personal data securely (shredding).

Maintain Data Accuracy: Report any inaccuracies in personal data you encounter.

Report Incidents: Immediately report any suspected data breach, privacy incident, or violation of policy to [Designated Contact/Team, e.g., IT Security or Legal/Compliance].

Think Before You Click/Share: Always consider the privacy implications of your actions, especially when dealing with personal data.
